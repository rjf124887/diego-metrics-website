Why Traditional Productivity Metrics Miss What Matters

Companies are seeing 300% increases in AI-generated output but flat revenues. Traditional metrics can't distinguish between activity and impact.

Activity Theater

Measuring hours worked, meetings attended, and emails sent while missing actual business value creation.

AI Productivity Illusion

AI generates high-volume output that inflates traditional metrics without creating proportional business impact.

Decision Bottlenecks

Teams appear "busy" while critical decisions stall and execution velocity decreases across the organization.

Introducing the DIEGO Framework

Five metrics that separate AI theater from business impact, giving executives clear visibility into what drives results.

D

Decisions

Velocity and quality of decision-making processes across the organization.

I

Impact

Business outcomes and value creation versus activity volume and busy work.

E

Execution

Speed from decision to measurable results and implementation effectiveness.

G

Growth

Capability building, knowledge transfer, and organizational scaling efficiency.

O

Operations

Focus time, collaboration quality, and resource utilization optimization.
